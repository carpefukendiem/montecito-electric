About the workshop

A quiet craft, practiced on the California coast since 1985.

We are a family-run, locally owned electrical contractor. Our workshop is small on purpose — which is how we've kept the quality high and the relationships long.

The owner's note

Forty years of showing up.

Montecito Electric is your locally owned and operated one-stop, full-service electrical repair company. Since 1985, I have been supplying the Santa Barbara and Montecito community with the highest quality residential and commercial services available.

I have always strived to provide quality work and the best customer service in the industry. It is my goal to consistently supply complete home and business comfort to my clients. My dedication extends far beyond my services — customer satisfaction is absolutely essential when it comes to working in someone's home or office.

That's why I have made sure you will always receive great savings and quality workmanship. At Montecito Electric, no matter what your servicing needs may be, "One Call Does It All." I will be the only call you'll ever have to make for your electrical needs.
