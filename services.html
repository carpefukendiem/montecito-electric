Services

Complete electrical craft, residential to commercial.

Every Montecito Electric job — from a buzzing outlet to a full-property rewire — is performed by our own licensed hands. Permitted, coded, and finished to a standard worthy of Montecito.

01
Diagnostics

Complete Home Electrical Troubleshooting & Repair

With our commitment to safety, we offer a complete home electrical troubleshooting and repair service. Flickering lights, dead outlets, mysterious breaker trips, burning smells — we systematically trace the fault, explain what's happening in plain English, and repair to code.

  • Dead circuits & outlets
  • Flickering or dimming lights
  • Frequent breaker trips
  • Burning or warm outlets
  • Whole-home safety inspection
  • Aluminum wiring remediation
02
Safety critical

Circuit Breakers & Panel Work

Often, when encountering over-current or short circuit, some units will fail to trip in response. This failure can result in fire, property damage, or personal injury. We replace tired breakers, upgrade undersized panels, and install sub-panels for additions and workshops.

  • Breaker replacement
  • 100A, 200A & 400A panel upgrades
  • Sub-panel installation
  • AFCI/GFCI breaker installs
  • Surge protection (whole-home)
  • Code-compliance upgrades
03
Code compliance

GFCI Replacement

Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters (GFCI) protect your family and coworkers from potentially deadly shocks due to the combination of wet ground and internal shorts in appliances. Required in kitchens, bathrooms, laundries, garages, and exteriors — we test, replace, and bring to current code.

  • Kitchen & bath receptacles
  • Exterior & patio outlets
  • Pool & spa circuits
  • Garage & laundry protection
  • Monthly testing guidance
  • Full inspection reports
04
Everyday essentials

Outlets, Switches & Gate Power

With technology the way it is today, it seems there's always a new gadget to bring home or into the office. We add, relocate, and upgrade receptacles and switches — and provide dedicated power & communication lines to gate systems. (We do not install the gate hardware itself.)

  • Added & relocated outlets
  • USB-C integrated receptacles
  • Dimmers & smart switches
  • Dedicated 20A circuits
  • Power to automatic gates
  • Low-voltage communication

After sundown

Landscape Lighting

  • Pathway & step lighting
  • Architectural up/down wash
  • Tree & canopy moonlighting
  • Smart-zoned transformers
  • Timer & photocell controls
  • LED retrofit of existing systems
